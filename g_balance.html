Balance is an equal distribution of weight. Symmetry is the term used to describe balance when the same elements exist on either side of a central axis. The Diné Bracelet, also on this Web site, is symmetrical. Asymmetrical balance occurs when unlike elements having equal weight are placed opposite each other. The upper section of the Bandolier Bag, also on this Web site, is asymmetrical. The lower portion is symmetrical.
